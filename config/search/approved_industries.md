### Technology

Companies whose primary business is producing or selling technology products
or services.

Examples (non-exhaustive): SaaS, cloud platforms, developer tools, consumer
internet, enterprise software, hardware/semiconductor, cybersecurity vendors,
AI/ML platforms, IT services and consulting.

### Healthcare

Companies operating in healthcare delivery, health technology, biotech, or
pharmaceuticals.

Examples (non-exhaustive): hospitals and health systems, digital health,
telemedicine, medical devices, biotech, pharma, health insurance technology,
clinical research.

### Finance

Companies in financial services or financial infrastructure.

Examples (non-exhaustive): banks, payments, capital markets, insurance,
asset management, fintech, crypto/blockchain financial services.

### Education

Companies whose primary business is education or learning.

Examples (non-exhaustive): edtech platforms, online learning, K-12 and
higher-ed software vendors, tutoring and training services, learning content
publishers.

### Government / Public Sector

Government agencies and contractors serving the public sector.

Examples (non-exhaustive): federal/state/local government, defense
contractors, civic technology vendors, public-sector consulting.
